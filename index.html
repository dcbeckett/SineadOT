Welcome to Sinead Edwards OT

Based in CastleBaldwin, County Sligo, we provide professional, person-centered occupational therapy services tailored to your individual needs.

At Sinead Edwards OT, we believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to live their life to the fullest. Our private occupational therapy services are designed to help individuals of all ages overcome challenges, regain independence, and achieve their personal goals. Whether you're recovering from an injury, managing a chronic condition, or seeking to improve your daily functioning, we're here to support you every step of the way.
