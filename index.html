Welcome to Sinead Edwards OT

Based in Castlebaldwin, County Sligo, we provide Paediatric Occupational Therapy services tailored to support children and their families.

The practice provides evidence based Paediatric Occupational Therapy assessment and intervention. In Occupational Therapy the term Occupation refers to the everyday activities that are meaningful and important in a child's life. For children, these occupations include play, learning, self-care, social interaction, rest and participation in family and community life. Occupational Therapy supports children to participate in these activities in ways that feel enjoyable, meaningful and supportive at every stage of their development. While recognising and honouring each individual child's strengths, we work collaboratively with families to build confidence, helping to support functional meaningful participation at home and school. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential.
