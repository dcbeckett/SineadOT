Welcome to Sinead Edwards OT

Based in CastleBaldwin, County Sligo, we provide specialized paediatric occupational therapy services tailored to support children and their families.

At Sinead Edwards OT, we believe that every child deserves the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential. Our private paediatric occupational therapy services are designed to help children overcome developmental challenges, build essential life skills, and participate fully in daily activities. Whether your child needs support with sensory processing, fine motor skills, self-care activities, or social participation, we're here to support your family every step of the way.
