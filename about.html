About Sinead Edwards

Experienced Occupational Therapist dedicated to improving lives

Meet Sinead Edwards

A passionate occupational therapist committed to helping individuals achieve independence and enhance their quality of life.

Education & Qualifications

Sinead studied Occupational Therapy at Trinity College, Dublin, one of Ireland's most prestigious institutions. Her comprehensive training at Trinity equipped her with a deep understanding of human anatomy, physiology, and the therapeutic interventions that can transform lives.

Professional Experience

With extensive experience in the public healthcare sector, Sinead has worked with the Health Service Executive (HSE), where she provided vital support to countless individuals facing physical, cognitive, and emotional challenges. Her time in the public system gave her invaluable insight into the diverse needs of patients across all age groups and conditions.

Throughout her career, Sinead has developed expertise in helping people recover from injuries, manage chronic conditions, and adapt their environments to maintain independence. Her compassionate approach focuses on understanding each person's unique circumstances and developing tailored solutions that make a real difference in their daily lives.

Areas of Expertise

Sinead is highly skilled in a wide range of occupational therapy methodologies, including:

  • Person-Centered Practice: Placing the individual at the heart of all therapeutic interventions
  • Environmental Modifications: Assessing and adapting homes for safety and accessibility
  • Neurological Rehabilitation: Supporting recovery from stroke, brain injury, and neurological conditions
  • Functional Assessment: Evaluating daily living skills and independence levels
  • Assistive Technology: Recommending equipment and aids to enhance independence
  • Falls Prevention: Implementing strategies to reduce risk and increase safety
  • Cognitive Rehabilitation: Supporting individuals with memory and cognitive challenges
  • Splinting & Hand Therapy: Custom solutions for hand conditions and injuries

Why Choose Private Occupational Therapy?

After years of dedicated service in the public healthcare system, Sinead established her private practice to offer more personalized, flexible care. Private occupational therapy allows for:

  • More time for comprehensive assessments
  • Flexible scheduling to suit your needs
  • Personalized treatment plans tailored specifically to you
  • Continuity of care with the same therapist throughout your journey
  • Home visits and assessments in your own environment

Philosophy of Care

Sinead believes that everyone deserves to live with dignity, independence, and purpose. Her approach combines clinical expertise with genuine compassion, ensuring that each client receives the highest standard of care in a supportive and understanding environment. Whether you're recovering from an illness, adapting to changes in your abilities, or seeking to optimize your daily functioning, Sinead is here to guide and support you every step of the way.
