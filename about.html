About Sinead Edwards

Specialist Paediatric Occupational Therapist dedicated to helping children thrive

Meet Sinead Edwards

A passionate paediatric occupational therapist committed to helping children achieve their developmental goals and reach their full potential.

Education & Qualifications

Sinead studied Occupational Therapy at Trinity College, Dublin, one of Ireland's most prestigious institutions. Her comprehensive training at Trinity equipped her with a deep understanding of child development, sensory processing, and the therapeutic interventions that can transform children's lives.

Professional Experience

With extensive experience in the public healthcare sector, Sinead has worked with the Health Service Executive (HSE), where she provided vital support to countless children and families facing developmental, sensory, and functional challenges. Her time in the public system gave her invaluable insight into the diverse needs of children with various conditions including autism spectrum disorder, developmental coordination disorder, sensory processing difficulties, and other developmental delays.

Throughout her career, Sinead has developed specialized expertise in paediatric occupational therapy, helping children develop essential skills for play, learning, and daily living. Her compassionate, child-centered approach focuses on understanding each child's unique strengths and challenges, working collaboratively with families to develop tailored interventions that make a real difference in children's daily lives.

Areas of Expertise

Sinead is highly skilled in a wide range of paediatric occupational therapy methodologies, including:

  • Child-Centered Practice: Placing the child and family at the heart of all therapeutic interventions
  • Sensory Integration Therapy: Helping children process and respond to sensory information effectively
  • Fine Motor Development: Supporting handwriting, cutting, drawing, and manipulation skills
  • Gross Motor Coordination: Enhancing balance, coordination, and motor planning abilities
  • Self-Care Skills: Building independence in dressing, eating, grooming, and toileting
  • Play-Based Therapy: Using play as a therapeutic tool to develop essential skills
  • School Readiness: Preparing children for the demands of the classroom environment
  • Social Skills Development: Supporting peer interaction and social participation
  • Assistive Technology: Recommending adaptive equipment and tools for children
  • Parent Training & Education: Empowering families with strategies to support their child

Why Choose Private Paediatric Occupational Therapy?

After years of dedicated service in the public healthcare system, Sinead established her private practice to offer more personalized, flexible care for children and families. Private paediatric occupational therapy allows for:

  • More time for comprehensive developmental assessments
  • Flexible scheduling to suit your family's needs
  • Personalized treatment plans tailored specifically to your child
  • Continuity of care with the same therapist throughout your child's journey
  • Home and school visits to support your child in their natural environments
  • Regular parent consultations and training sessions

Philosophy of Care

Sinead believes that every child has unique strengths and the potential to thrive. Her approach combines clinical expertise with genuine compassion and a playful spirit, ensuring that each child receives therapy that is engaging, meaningful, and fun. She works collaboratively with families, educators, and other professionals to create a supportive network around each child. Whether your child is working on sensory regulation, developing motor skills, building independence, or improving social participation, Sinead is here to guide and support your family every step of the way.
