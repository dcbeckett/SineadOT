About Sinead Edwards

Private Paediatric Occupational Therapist

Meet Sinead Edwards

Education & Professional experience

Sinead studied Occupational Therapy at Trinity College, qualifying in 2015. Since then, Sinead has gained extensive experience working in the area of paediatrics within the Health Care Executive (HSE), supporting children and their families facing developmental, sensory and functional challenges.

Professional Experience

With extensive experience in the public healthcare sector, Sinead has gained experience supporting children with a range of neurodivergent profiles, including Autism, DCD and sensory processing difficulties.

Sinead's work is grounded in a neuroaffirming approach that values neurodiversity, honouring each child's individuality. Therapy is collaborative, strengths focused and tailored to help each child participate meaningfully in daily life.

Why Choose Private Paediatric Occupational Therapy?

Choosing private Occupational Therapy offers families timely, personalised support tailored to each child's individual needs and the environment to create a strong collaborative relationship between therapist, the child and their family. A neuro-affirming approach means your child is supported in ways that respect who they are, build on their strengths, allowing them to participate more confidently in everyday activities at home, school and in the community.
