Our Services

Comprehensive occupational therapy solutions tailored to your needs

Our clinical specialist occupational therapy services are designed to help you maintain independence, enhance your quality of life, and overcome daily challenges with confidence.

Housing Adaptation Reports

Our comprehensive housing adaptation assessments evaluate your home environment and identify modifications that can enhance safety, accessibility, and independence. Whether you need minor adjustments such as grab rails and bath equipment or major alterations including stairlifts and complete bathroom renovations, we provide detailed reports and recommendations tailored to your specific needs. These assessments are essential for grant applications and ensuring your home supports your long-term wellbeing.

Equipment Assessment

We provide expert guidance on selecting the right assistive equipment to support your daily activities. Our assessments cover everything from mobility aids and bath safety equipment to specialized seating solutions and wheelchairs. We take the time to understand your unique requirements and recommend equipment that not only meets your functional needs but also fits comfortably within your lifestyle and home environment.

Independent Living Assessments

Our independent living assessments are comprehensive evaluations designed to support you in living safely and confidently in your own home. We assess your ability to perform daily activities, identify potential risks, and develop strategies to maintain your independence for as long as possible. These assessments are particularly valuable for older adults, individuals with progressive conditions, or those preparing to transition back home after a hospital stay.

Lifestyle Redesign Services

Life changes, whether due to aging, illness, or injury, often require us to rethink how we approach our daily routines. Our lifestyle redesign services help you adapt to these changes by developing new strategies and habits that support your wellbeing and goals. We work with you to identify meaningful activities, overcome barriers, and create a sustainable plan for living well in the present while preparing thoughtfully for the future.
